# Feature Ideas

## Data & Insights

- **Export/Backup** - Export entries to PDF, markdown, or JSON (share with therapist, personal backup)
- **AI Weekly Summary** - Use the Anthropic API to generate a weekly/monthly reflection summary of themes
- **Word clouds / Analytics** - Visualize common themes, sentiment trends over time
- **Related entries** - When viewing an entry, show similar past entries based on content

## Daily Experience

- **Reminders** - Push notification at a preferred time to journal
- **Home screen widget** - Show today's streak or a prompt to complete
- **Mood tracking** - Quick mood selection before/after completing (track correlation over time)
- **Voice input** - Speak your completion instead of typing

## Organization

- **Favorites/Stars** - Mark meaningful entries for quick access
- **Custom tags** - Add personal tags to entries for filtering
- **Search filters** - Filter history by date range, category, mood, tags
- **Custom stems** - Let users create their own stems

## Engagement

- **Stem ratings** - Rate stems after completing ("This resonated" / "Not for me") to improve future suggestions
- **Goals** - Set weekly completion goals with progress tracking
- **Entry reactions** - Come back to past entries and add a reaction/follow-up note

## Sharing & Social

- **Share as image** - Generate a nice card image of an entry to share
- **Cloud sync** - Sync across devices with end-to-end encryption

## Quality of Life

- **Undo delete** - Recover recently deleted entries
- **Entry editing** - Edit past completions (with history)
- **Keyboard shortcuts** - For desktop power users
